2. 🗺️ Our Proposed Phased Roadmap

We've structured the development into a logical, modular roadmap. We begin with the highest-impact features to deliver immediate value, building a robust foundation for all subsequent capabilities.

1
AI Findings Generator

Build the core engine to automate the first draft of the "Findings" summary for each health domain.

  • Synthesizes client test results, profile data, physician input, and goals.
  • Integrates a curated knowledge base (e.g., Peter Attia's frameworks).
  • Establishes the "human-in-the-loop" workflow for physician review and refinement.

➔
2
AI Action Plan Generator

Leverage the AI-generated findings to create a deeply personalized and actionable client plan.

  • Maps findings to critical intervention categories (nutrition, exercise, supplements).
  • Generates personalized content *within* action cards, tailored to client context.
  • Includes confidence scores to help physicians prioritize their review.

➔
3
The Longevity Assistant

Deploy an intelligent, client-facing chatbot that is aware of the client's personal data.

  • Acts as a Q&A agent for questions about health domains and action plans.
  • Allows clients to update their profile via chat (e.g., "I started strength training").
  • Proactively assists with reminders and can serve as a communication filter for the team.

➔
4
Intake & Consult Recording AI

Streamline consultations by automatically processing audio sessions to save significant administrative time.

  • Accurately transcribes audio from intake and follow-up consultations.
  • Automatically generates a concise summary and key takeaways for review.
  • Suggests profile updates and to-do items for the physician based on the conversation.

➔
5
The Internal Team Assistant

Enhance operational efficiency with an internal AI for the MyActiveAge team.

  • Helps coordinate and schedule appointments by interfacing with calendars.
  • Acts as a first-line filter for incoming client messages, handling common queries.
  • Assists in developing professional course content by leveraging the knowledge base.
